Aksel

Aksel is from a small island between Bergen and Stavanger named Bømlo.
He was born August 20th, 1998, and last year he went to Jæren Folkehøgskule and studied technology there.
While at Jæren Folkehøgskule, he learned about programming, and has some experience in programming.
Aksel has always been interested i computers and technology, and has made his own robot as well as made his own phone cover.
In his spare time Aksel enjoys playing football, volleyball or playing movie music on his trumpet.
Aksel loves traveling the world, and has been to places like Rome, the Caribbean, Singapore and Malaysia.
