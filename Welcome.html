Welcome

This is the webpage for the subject ING102 regarding the project KHapp.
Khapp is an app made to make living in a collective easier by introducing a smart to do list.
This to do list make it easier for the people that are sharing living space to communicate what needs to be done.
For example: buy milk or clean the oven.
This app is being developed by four people; Kristoffer, Sølve, Bjørnar and Aksel.
To read more about each person and their qualities, click on their name box at the top left of your screen.
